The team:

The team we formed for this competition is named "Ares," drawing inspiration from the mighty War God, specifically the embodiment of the spirit of battle. We selected this name to symbolize the trajectory that a missile follows during a wartime conflict.

Why?

We embarked on this project with the goal of assisting individuals with their challenges before they enter university.

How?

In addition to creating the calculator, we are currently engaged in this project, and we have plans for a future endeavor aimed at providing comprehensive explanations of various topics.

Benefits

Our team derives personal satisfaction from this project, while students benefit from being thoroughly prepared on the subject matter.
