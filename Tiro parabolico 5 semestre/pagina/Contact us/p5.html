Input Values of the calculator:

1. Mass of the ball (m).

2. Gravity (g) which may vary in magnitude.

3. Spring constant (k) Newton/meter.

4. Height from ground to target (hf), which may vary from an imaginary reference line. The values ​​may be positive or negative with respect to that line.

5. Height of the trigger with respect to the floor (ho) that may vary with respect to the same imaginary reference line of the aim. The values ​​may be positive or negative with respect to that line. It is the height at which the ball leaves the trigger.

6. Horizontal distance from the mechanism to the center of the objective (L).

7. Coordinates of an obstacle (x, y), which should be avoided when making your shot.
